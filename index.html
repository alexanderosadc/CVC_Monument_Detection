About Us

Great ideas won’t happen in a vacuum. To break out of that rut, we just need to zoom the perspective to a macro level and discover new viewpoints. The Computer Vision Camp gives the opportunity to see a bigger picture that, eventually, will diagnose problems and fulfill the purpose.

A close-knit team was created thanks to the camp, AIV (An eye Visionaries), which is filled to the brim with creativity, passion and enthusiasm. It consists of 4 young changemakers: Boris Bîtca, Vitalie Furdui, Dan Oprea, and Tatiana Poverjuc, that are beyond lucky to be mentored by the fabulous leader, Alex Osadcenco. That small, but mighty team has thought about things they encounter and pop up with the idea to develop a tourist-friendly app from scratch. "Tell me what is it? Tourist Edition" is an application for detecting cultural monuments and displaying information about them.

Fingers crossed and let’s rock!
